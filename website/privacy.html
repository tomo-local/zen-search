Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Chrome/Firefox extension "Zen Search" (the "Extension") handles user information. Please read this policy carefully before using the Extension.

2. Information We Access and How We Use It

The Extension accesses the following information solely within your local browser environment. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.

  • Browsing history: Used to provide search results from your browser history.
  • Tab information: Used to display and search your currently open tabs.
  • Bookmarks: Used to include bookmark results in search.

All processing happens entirely on your device.

3. Data Sharing

The Extension does not share any user information with third parties. No data is collected, stored on external servers, or sold.

4. Security

Because the Extension processes all information locally, the risk of data leakage via external network access is minimal. However, the security of your own device is your responsibility. We recommend keeping your operating system and browser up to date.

5. Your Rights

You can stop the Extension from accessing your data at any time by uninstalling it from your browser. You can also delete browsing history, tabs, and bookmarks directly through your browser's built-in features.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via GitHub Issues or email tomohiro.togashi@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
